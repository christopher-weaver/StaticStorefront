Useful Information

Check movies & times: they change daily!

The price of admission is $11.00 for individuals age 13 years and up. Kids 4 years to 12 years old are $6.00 each.

Box office opens at 7:30PM on Friday & Saturday and 8:00PM on Sunday, Monday & Thursday.

We cannot offer refunds for bad weather.

Please arrive early on Friday and Saturday nights. The lines to get into our drive-in can get rather long, especially close to showtime.

Movies start at dusk.

Movie sound comes through your FM radio. You must have a working radio or boom box to hear the movie. We do NOT have radios available for you, so please bring your own.

Thursday night showings are open-caption when available.

No grills or open fires are allowed.

Absolutely NO fireworks allowed.

You are welcome to bring your lawn chair but we ask you to setup in front of your vehicle for safety and so other patrons have the ability to park their cars as well.

If you have a concern or if you are experiencing car trouble, you can ask for help at the concession stand or ask one of our friendly lot attendants. We will do what we can to get your car running again. Between movies, please start your car for a few minutes to make sure you don’t drain your car battery.

We are pround of the Tibbs and work very hard to keep it safe and clean. You can help by using the trash bags we give you at the gate and the trash cans available around the building.
